BeachHacks 2021

I volunteered at BeachHacks this last spring. It was my first time and hope to do it again. It was virtual due to Covid but usually has a great turnout. It is organized by ACM (Association for Computing Machinery) I did a one hour workshop on beginning front end web using GitHub for hosting. This is usually on campus and students get to stay on campus for 24hrs to complete a challenge. Looking forward to attending in person next year.
